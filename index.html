Sexuality Flags and LGBT+ Symbols - Bi Flag

The rainbow flag, or bi flag which represents gay pride, is well-known to all. However, there are many flags that can be used by the LGBTQ+ community to represent the diverse range of gender identities and sexual orientations.

Why are there so many flags representing the different groups in the community?

Monica Helms, creator of the Transgender Pride Flag probably said it best when she stated, "I say that the rainbow flag looks like the American flag: everyone's under it." Each group has its own flag, just like every state.

Why are flags so symbolic? Gilbert Baker, the creator of the first gay pride flag, stated that flags communicate something. Put a rainbow flag on the windshield to say something.
